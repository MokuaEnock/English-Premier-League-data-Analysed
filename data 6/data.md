About Dataset
Fixture list for the upcoming season of the English Premier League footy. Information accurate at time of scraping (07/14/2022). Subject to change.

date: match date
kickoffTime: scheduled match kick-off time
homeTeam: home team
awayTeam: away team
venue: venue
